Low-poly stylized 3D

True free-roam 3D (Three.js): walk anywhere with WASD + mouse-look, day-lit flat-shaded buildings, floating location labels, press E at a door to see that building's details. Looks like a cozy indie game. My recommendation — best wow-factor that's realistically buildable.

Retro 90s dungeon-crawler

Doom/Daggerfall vibes: chunky pixel textures, flat sprite NPCs that always face you, moody palette, monospace HUD. Free-roam but deliberately lo-fi — nostalgia is the aesthetic, and the low fidelity hides a lot of art effort.

Myst-style point & click

First-person but not free-roam: a set of beautiful pre-made scenes (AI-generated or painted), one per spot in town. Click arrows to step between viewpoints, click buildings to open details. Least code and can look gorgeous — but movement is hop-by-hop, not walking.
